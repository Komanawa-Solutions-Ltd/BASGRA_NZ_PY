Days Harvest Keys
Key, Unit, Description 
'year', , year e.g. 2002
'doy', , day of year 1 - 356 (366 for leap year)
'frac_harv', fraction ,  fraction (0-1) of material above target to harvest to maintain 'backward capabilities' with v2.0.0
'harv_trig', kgDM/ha , dm above which to initiate harvest, if trigger is less than zero no harvest will take place
'harv_targ', kgDM/ha , dm to harvest to or to remove depending on 'fixed_removal'
'weed_dm_frac', fraction ,  fraction of dm of ryegrass to attribute to weeds
'reseed_trig',  fraction , when BASAL <= reseed_trig, trigger a reseeding. if <0 then do not reseed
'reseed_basal', fraction , set BASAL = reseed_basal when reseeding.
